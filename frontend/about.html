Behind the fabric

A story woven by hand.

From flax fields in Normandy to merino farms in the highlands, we partner with small mills that have refined their craft over generations. Every meter is traceable, every dye low-impact.

Sustainability

We source organic cotton, responsibly grown linen, and recycled blends wherever possible. Our packaging is plastic-free and our dyes are OEKO-TEX certified.

Our atelier

Founded in 1974, Maison Lin began as a family weaving studio. Today we serve makers across India and worldwide — with prices in rupees and shipping from our Mumbai warehouse.
